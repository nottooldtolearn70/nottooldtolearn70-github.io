Peach Cobbler Recipe

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Self-rising flour 1 cup
Peaches 1 cup

Melt 1 stick of margarine in 9 x 13 glass pan in overn on 350 degrees while you mixing ingredients together. In mixing bowl mix self-rising flour, sugar and milk together. After margarine melts take out of oven. Add peaches to mixture and pour in pan. Cook for an hour or until brown. Remove from oven. Serve hot or let cool and then serve. It is also great with ice cream or milk.

Deviled Eggs Recipe

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Mayo 2 tbsp
Salt Pinch
Pepper Pinch

Boil eggs for 15 minutes. Peel eggs, cut in half. Put yoke in bowl and mix Mayo, salt and pepper. Spoon yoke mixture in the egg whites. You may also salad/cube pickles and mustard to the yokes.

Sweet Tea Recipe

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 2 cups
Family size tea bags 4

Put tea bags in micro safe 16 cup glass bowl or measuring bowl with 12 cups of water. Micro on high for 8 minutes. In a gallon tea pitcher add sugar and microwaved tea. Sir, top off with water and sir again.
